Mimi na Jamii Foundation

Mimi na Jamii Foundation is a youth-led movement based in Tanzania, committed to empowering local communities through transformative initiatives in health, education, and technology. We work to uplift marginalized groups and promote sustainable development by providing access to essential resources and opportunities. Officially registered under the Ministry of Community Development, Gender, Women and Special Groups through the Office of the Registrar of Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs), with registration number 00NGO/R/4263.

Believing in the fresh perspectives and potential of youth, Mimi na Jamii Foundation brings together young people in Tanzania to take part in community development in solving problems facing themselves and empowering communities around them.

Our foundation strives to make a lasting impact by fostering positive change in underserved areas. By focusing on health, education, and technology, we aim to improve lives, create lasting solutions, and provide a platform for youth to lead with innovation and compassion. Through collaborative efforts, we are building a brighter future for Tanzania and beyond, one community at a time.

Our Mission

To empower youth in Tanzania to drive community development through initiatives in health, education, and technology, fostering sustainable progress and social impact.

Our Vision

A future where young people are the driving force behind inclusive, innovative, and sustainable community solutions; creating a society where every individual has access to education, digital opportunities, and a healthier life.

Core Values

  • Youth-Led Impact: We believe in the power of young people to lead change and actively participate in solving challenges affecting their communities.
  • Inclusivity & Equity: We advocate for equal opportunities in education, digital access, and social support, ensuring no one is left behind, regardless of background or ability.
  • Innovation & Sustainability: We embrace technology and creative solutions to address societal challenges while ensuring long-term, community-driven change.

Innovation

We embrace technology and innovative solutions to create lasting social impact. By integrating creative and sustainable approaches, we strive to lead the way in community-driven change.

Bring Them Back to Life

Mimi na Jamii collaborates with local rehabilitation centers to provide food, necessities, and emotional support for recovering drug addicts. The initiative ensures dignity and care during their rehabilitation journey.

School Outreach Programs on Mental Health Awareness

Mimi na Jamii Foundation works with schools to raise awareness about mental health issues affecting youth, providing support programs and resources to help them cope.

Connecting Families with Rehab Services

We help families struggling with addiction by connecting them with rehabilitation services, offering guidance, support, and assistance in navigating the recovery process.

Leadership Training Seminars for High School Students

Our leadership seminars help high school students develop essential leadership skills, empowering them to become proactive change-makers in their communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Mimi na Jamii Foundation offer?

Mimi na Jamii Foundation offers a variety of community-centered services focused on youth empowerment, mental health awareness, education, digital literacy, addiction recovery, and leadership training. We aim to improve the well-being of underserved communities.

How can I join or volunteer with Mimi na Jamii Foundation?

We welcome volunteers from all walks of life. You can join us by signing up on our website, attending our events, or following us on social media for updates. We encourage youth, students, and professionals to participate and make a difference.

How can I support Mimi na Jamii Foundation?

You can support us through donations, volunteering, or partnership opportunities. Your contributions help us run programs that positively impact the lives of youth, families, and communities in need. Reach out to us for more details on how you can get involved.

Where does Mimi na Jamii Foundation operate?

Mimi na Jamii Foundation operates across Tanzania, with a strong focus on community outreach in schools, rehabilitation centers, digital literacy training hubs, and more. We are working to empower people throughout the country and create positive change in every community we reach.

Is Mimi na Jamii Foundation a registered organization?

Yes, Mimi na Jamii Foundation is a legally recognized non-governmental organization (NGO) operating in Tanzania. We are fully compliant with all national regulations and work under the guidelines set by the government and relevant authorities.

Can organizations or companies collaborate with Mimi na Jamii Foundation?

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with other organizations, corporations, and government institutions. By working together, we can expand our reach and create more meaningful impact in areas like education, health, and technology. Contact us for collaboration opportunities.

How do I stay updated on Mimi na Jamii Foundationâ€™s activities?

Stay updated by following us on social media, subscribing to our newsletter, or regularly visiting our website. We provide regular updates on our events, programs, and initiatives through these platforms.

How do I contact Mimi na Jamii Foundation?

You can contact us via email, phone, or visit our office in person. More details can be found on our "Contact Us" page on the website. We are always happy to assist and answer any inquiries you may have.
